How to play

Pairs Game

To play this game you don't need a controller, you only need a mouse to click if on a desktop playform. However if you are viewing this site on a mobile or tablet device you can use your finger to click with. There a 12 cards in total and 6 pairs to match within those 12 cards. You can take as much time as you want and your moves are counted once you match all 6 pairs together. You can restart the game at whatever time you want, even without finishing the game and restart. I hope you enjoy the game and press the "ready" button below to start the game.
